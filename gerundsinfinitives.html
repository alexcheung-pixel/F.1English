Bare infinitives

We use the base form of the verb after:

cancouldhave tomightmay
mustought toshouldmakelet
  • I can swim (swim).
  • You should study (study).
  • He must leave (leave) now.

Rule: DO NOT make any changes to the verbs after the above verbs.

Gerunds (V-ing)

We use v-ing:

Rule 1: When a verb is the subject of a sentence.

  • Swimming is fun.
  • Reading improves your English.
  • Playing football is exciting.

Rule 3: After prepositions (about, at, by, for, in, of, on, with, from, before, after).

  • She is good at drawing (draw).
  • He left without saying (say) goodbye.
  • After finishing (finish) homework, I watched TV.
